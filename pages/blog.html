Technology and Trends in the Watch Industry


Exploring how innovation is shaping the future of watches.

"A watch reminds us every second counts-make it worthwhile"


Rise of Technology in Watch Industry

    As technology is continuing to advancement, the watch industry is also exploring the technology and the appealing feature of the watches have made a huge change to watch entusiasts and manufacturers.
    A significant transformation have been observed and the smartwatches have become very popular recently. Different features like voice assistant, fitness tracker, steps calculator, and many more have been added on smartwatches.
    Whereas, people can no even text and call from their smartwatches without their cellphone as well. They even consist of features like weather forecast and news. The rise of watch technology has been on its peak as all these features are available right from their wrist.
    Similarly, rise of e-commerce has been convenient for the consumers as they can buy their perfect watches online.
    People can even see the reviews of the consumers and make their final decision to shop online. Hence, technology is changing the watch industry in many ways and all these factors have contributed to the growth of watch industry from many years.


The Positive Impact of Technology in Watch Industry

    Smartwatches have become an wearable technology which has both sustainability and style. They are used to track our heart rate, blood pressure, fitness levels and many more. The most effective part of smartwatches is that it helps the consumers to stay motivated and exercise on a regular basis. Similarly, smartwatches help to detect symptoms of different heart disease and even helped consumers on the pandemic situation of COVID-19. Due to features of smartwatch apps like weather forecast and news, etc are also available on any consumer's wrist. Without the help of our personal cellphones, smartwatches have allowed us to make phone calls and text messages. It has become a very convenient product of a consumer for their day to day life without any other devices.


The Negative Impact of Technology in Watch Industry

    Smartwatches have many positive impacts but they come with many drawbacks as well. Smartwatches doesn't have the lifespan of decades like the traditional watches and its maintenance is also very low. They have the lifespan of only a few years due to the factors like battery degradation and rapid speed of technology advancement. Disposal of smartwatches are also not efficient as they contain non-biodegradable products. Traditional watches consist of craftmanship while smartwatches don't.



Consumer Trends

    Today's consumers are looking for watches that has the perfect combination of both style and functionality. As people's lifestyle has become very dynamic, they look for watches that has simple designs but also elegant which helps the consumers to complement wide range of outfits. In the same way, functionality is also very important. Watches featuring health-focused technology is a go-to material for health conscious individual. The GPS tracker available on Smartwatches helps people to navigate and measure travelled distance helping the individual to stay active. Affordabilty is also a very important factor that is considered by today's buyers. While advanced features and looks are also admired, many people cannot afford them. So, many consumers are drawn to brands that have appealing appearance and affodable.
